## Option 2: Vercel (Alternative)

1. Go to: https://vercel.com/new
2. Click "Import Git Repository"
3. Select `TrippyStockTicker`
4. Click "Deploy"
5. Done! Your site will be live at: `trippy-stock-ticker-xxx.vercel.app`
